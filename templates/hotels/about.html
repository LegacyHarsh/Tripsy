About Travel Explorer

Our Story

Tripsy was founded in 2025 with a simple mission: to make travel planning easier, more affordable, and more enjoyable for everyone.

What started as a small project to organize our own trips has grown into a full-featured web app designed to make planning smooth, collaborative, and stress-free. Whether you're flying solo or coordinating with friends, Tripsy keeps everything in one place—so you can focus on the journey, not the chaos.
